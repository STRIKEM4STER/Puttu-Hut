About Us

Puttu Hut brings the authentic taste of Kerala to your table, specializing in Puttu, a traditional South Indian delicacy. With a focus on fresh ingredients and a passion for cooking, we offer a variety of dishes that celebrate the rich flavors of our heritage. Each meal at Puttu Hut is crafted with care to give you a memorable and delicious dining experience. Our warm and welcoming atmosphere ensures that every visit feels like home. Whether you're a long-time lover of South Indian cuisine or trying it for the first time, Puttu Hut offers something special for everyone.
